Our Team

Meet the dedicated individuals who keep the Purdue Hot Air Balloon Club soaring!

  • President: Leads the club, oversees operations, and ensures that the club achieves its goals while fostering a welcoming environment for all members.
  • Vice President: Supports the President, coordinates events, and manages internal communication between different roles and members.
  • Treasurer: Manages the club’s finances, budgets for events, and ensures financial transparency for all activities.
  • Secretary: Handles meeting minutes, keeps club records, and ensures smooth communication with members and external parties.
  • Safety Officer: Ensures all flights and ground operations meet safety standards and educates members on proper safety procedures.
  • Faculty Advisor(s): Provides guidance and expertise, leveraging their knowledge from the School of Engineering or Pro Flight program.
  • Head Pilot: Oversees flight operations, coordinates with pilots, and ensures all flights are conducted professionally and efficiently.
  • Pilots: Certified members responsible for operating the hot air balloons during events and club activities.
  • Head Ground Crew: Leads the ground team in setting up, chasing, and recovering the hot air balloons during flights.
  • Balloon Technician: Maintains and inspects all equipment, ensuring balloons are flight-ready and in optimal condition.
  • Meteorology Chair: Monitors weather conditions and provides critical data to pilots to ensure safe and successful flights.
  • Publicity Chair: Promotes the club through social media, campus events, and other outreach efforts to increase visibility and membership.
  • Fundraising Chair: Organizes fundraising initiatives to support club operations, events, and equipment maintenance.
